About Me

Emmet Tam is an undergraduate at the University of Maryland, College Park currently enrolled in the dual degree program in Computer Science and Psychology. He is also part of the scholars program, Science and Global Change, where he took courses dealing with climate change and its environmental and psychological impact on society. He is a passionate and motivated type of individual. He has worked multiple jobs ranging from hospitality to engineering and has experience taking part in multiple research projects at the University of Maryland, College Park. He is also an avid tennis and ping pong fan/player and hopes to make it to the big leagues one day.
